Prefer ripple rings over botanical/sprig motifs for the loader. The sprig felt off even with correct colors.

**Why:** The site has a nature theme but the sprig looked out of place as an animation. Ripple rings (concentric circles expanding and fading, like a drop in still water) felt more fitting — atmospheric and cinematic rather than literal.

**How to apply:** When suggesting or building loaders, transitions, or ambient animations for this site, lean toward abstract organic forms (ripples, glows, pulses) over representational botanical illustrations.
